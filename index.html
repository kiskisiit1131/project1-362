The IIT Reboots are a co-ed outreach based a cappella group that volunteers to perform around the city of Chicago. We rehearse once a week during the IIT lunch hour and perform on Sundays.

AUDITIONS

Prepare 30 seconds of any song of your choice or beatboxing beats that showcase your skills. Also, you'll need to learn the Star Spangled Banner in the key of your choice.

QUESTIONS

If you have any questions at all or have ideas for places we could perform at, please feel free to email me at akiskis@hawk.iit.edu. I check my email daily so expect a quick response!
